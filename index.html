About Our Legal Services

Wills & Estate Planning

Affordable preparation of Wills, Powers of Attorney and Appointments of Enduring Guardian. Wills are prepared visually and signed at the first meeting with our principal David Wilson, a post-graduate qualified Estate Planning Lawyer.

Comprehensive Estate Services

We act on all types of grants including general and special Grants for executors, administrators and beneficiaries. We act for executors and administrators no matter where they are; local, interstate or international. We do not charge percentage realisation fees on Estate Administration but reasonable hourly rates for work performed.

Estate Disputes

We act for those left out of an estate or for those not properly and adequately provided for. We also defend estates to uphold the terms of a will. We act in cases of disputed grants of probate and letters of Administration, Resolution & Litigation.

Death Benefit Claims

Our firm acts in Common Law Negligence Claims for wrongful death. We also act in claims for Workers Compensation Benefits in respect of fatal workplace accidents and work related deaths.
