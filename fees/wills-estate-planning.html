Complex Estate Planning

Some situations require more detailed planning and custom provisions. These include:

  • Children from previous relationships
  • Protected trusts for beneficiaries with special needs or concerns
  • Business, company, or family trust interests
  • Discretionary testamentary trusts for tax planning purposes
  • Blended family arrangements
  • International assets or beneficiaries

For these situations, we will provide a detailed cost estimate after our initial consultation once we understand your specific circumstances.
