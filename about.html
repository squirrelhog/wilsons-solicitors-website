Our Story

Wilsons Solicitors was founded in 1971 with a simple mission: provide honest, affordable, and effective legal services to the Penrith community.

Established 1971 — Over five decades later, we continue to uphold these founding principles. Our longevity in the legal profession is testament to our commitment to client satisfaction and our deep understanding of both local and state legal requirements.

Innovation in Legal Services — We've always believed in making legal services accessible and understandable. Our pioneering visual will preparation system ensures clients can see their will being created in real-time, eliminating confusion and ensuring complete understanding.

Clear Communication — This innovative approach extends to all our services, where we prioritize clear communication and transparent processes throughout every client relationship.
